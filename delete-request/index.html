Account Support

Data Deletion Request

Use this page to request deletion of your BroRocks app account and associated personal data, even if you already uninstalled the app.

How this works

  • Submit the form below, or email us directly at contact@brorocks.com.
  • Include your account email and username so we can locate your data.
  • We may ask for a quick ownership verification before deleting account data.
  • Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Account profile information (email, username, avatar).
  • User-generated content tied to your account where deletion is supported.
  • App activity data associated with your account.

Some records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

Submit Request

Submitting opens your default email app with a prefilled deletion request. If it does not open, email us at contact@brorocks.com.
