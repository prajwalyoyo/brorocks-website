Policy

Child Safety Standards

Zero Tolerance Policy

We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content that depicts or encourages the sexual exploitation of children (CSAM).

Reporting Tools

Our app provides in-app reporting tools (the "Report" button on every debate and comment) allowing users to flag any suspicious or harmful content immediately.

Moderation

All reported content is reviewed by our team within 24 hours. Any user found sharing illegal content will have their account permanently banned and their IP address blocked.

Legal Compliance

We cooperate fully with law enforcement and will report any instances of child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or relevant local authorities.
